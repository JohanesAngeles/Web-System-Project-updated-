My love for BTS and K-dramas began unexpectedly during the pandemic. To my surprise, I found myself laughing and thoroughly enjoying the performance. This reaction shocked me because, prior to this moment, I had never been interested in K-pop or K-dramas. That day, purely by accident, BTS brightened my day and sparked a newfound interest.

Watching these dramas became another source of comfort and entertainment during the long days of the pandemic. Through K-dramas, I discovered a new appreciation for Korean culture and entertainment, further enriching my pandemic experience.
