Before embarking on my weight loss journey, my lifestyle was far from healthy. I was eating unholy amounts of junk food, not paying any attention to nutritional value. Fast food and sugary snacks were my go-to, and I never gave a second thought to my health. My physical appearance became a source of ridicule, especially in high school where I faced relentless bullying. The bullying and my growing health concerns led me to become an out-of-school youth. I felt something was not right with my body, but I continued down a path of self-neglect and poor habits.

The turning point came when I realized that I needed to change for myself, not for others. Despite the bullying and external pressures, it was my internal desire for better health and self-confidence that fueled my decision. I wanted to feel good about myself and knew that a healthier lifestyle was essential. My health concerns were also a major factor; I could no longer ignore the signs that my body was giving me. It was a profound moment of realization that, as a human, I deserved to be confident and proud of who I am.

My weight loss journey began with implementing intermittent fasting, using a 12-hour eating window. I also made a conscious effort to eliminate toxic habits from my life. This included cutting out foods high in sugar and glucose, which were major culprits in my unhealthy diet. Exercise became a regular part of my routine, helping to boost my physical and mental health. One month into these changes, I started seeing results. The weight loss was noticeable, and more importantly, I began to feel beautiful and confident. This newfound confidence was a stark contrast to the self-doubt I had felt before.
